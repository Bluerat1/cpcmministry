Who We Are

Our Vision & Mission

🌟

Our Vision

To see every person behind bars encounter the living God and become a transformed disciple who impacts communities and plants churches — demonstrating that no life is beyond the reach of God's grace.

✝️

Our Mission

To evangelize, disciple, and equip prisoners and ex-PDLs (Persons Deprived of Liberty) through biblical training and holistic programs — restoring them to their families, communities, and to God.

Our Story

The History of CPCM

Community Prison Church Ministry (CPCM) was born out of a burning conviction that the Gospel is for everyone — including those the world has forgotten behind prison walls. What began as small, faith-filled visits to a local jail grew into a full-scale ministry touching thousands of lives across multiple correctional facilities in the Philippines.

Fueled by love, driven by Scripture, and sustained by the faithfulness of God, CPCM has become a beacon of hope for Persons Deprived of Liberty (PDLs) and their families. Many who once lived in darkness now lead churches, mentor others, and carry the Gospel forward as living testimonies of grace.

The Calling Begins

God places a burden on the hearts of the founders to bring the Gospel inside prison walls, starting with regular visitations and Bible studies.

First Discipleship Program

Systematic biblical training is introduced inside prison, raising up leaders among PDLs who begin teaching fellow inmates.

Beyond the Bars

The ministry expands to support ex-PDLs after release — livelihood programs, pastoral training, and reintegration assistance.

Churches Planted

Biblically trained ex-PDLs are commissioned to pastor and plant churches, multiplying the ministry's impact across communities.

Growing Impact

Today CPCM serves multiple jails nationwide with a growing army of volunteers, many of whom are transformed ex-PDLs themselves.

Our Heart

Why Share God's Love Behind Bars?

Prisoners are among the most forgotten people in society. Yet Jesus made it clear — visiting those in prison is an act of love done unto Him.

📖

God's Command

Scripture calls us to remember those in prison as though we ourselves were suffering with them (Hebrews 13:3). Obedience to this command is not optional — it is love in action.

♟️

Strategic Impact

Prisons are unreached communities inside our cities. Transformed prisoners go back to their barrios and become some of the most powerful witnesses for Christ in the most difficult places.

🕊️

No Life is Beyond Grace

The grace of God knows no bars. Every person in a cell is someone Jesus died for. CPCM exists to make sure they hear that message and are given a chance to respond.

"The Spirit of the Lord is on me, because he has anointed me… to proclaim freedom for the prisoners." — Luke 4:18
What We Do

Our Ministries

A holistic approach — reaching people inside prison walls and walking with them all the way to restoration.

📣

Evangelism

Regular Gospel proclamation inside jail facilities, reaching PDLs with the message of salvation through Jesus Christ.

📚

Discipleship

Systematic Bible study and spiritual formation for those who have accepted Christ, grounding them in the Word of God.

🎓

Biblical / Leadership Training

In-depth pastoral and leadership classes equipping PDLs to lead fellowships and eventually plant churches upon release.

🔧

Equipping Program

Continued training and mentorship for ex-PDLs to help them grow in faith and skills after their release.

✝️

Pastoral / Leadership Class

Ongoing theological and leadership education for ex-PDLs called to ministry and church planting.

🌾

Livelihood Program

Practical skills training and livelihood support to help ex-PDLs rebuild their lives and provide for their families.

👧

Children of Prisoners

Scholarship program and spiritual care for the children of those behind bars — ensuring the next generation is not left behind.

🏠

Halfway House

A safe transitional home for newly released ex-PDLs, providing shelter, discipleship, and life-skills coaching.

🕊️

Rebels Returnee Ministry

Dedicated outreach for former rebels and surrenderees, walking with them through reintegration and spiritual transformation.

📝

Values Education

Conducting character and values formation classes in schools and campuses to build moral foundations in the youth.

💊

Drug Awareness Symposiums

Partnering with schools to provide drug education through the lens of faith, prevention, and lasting transformation.

🛡️

Police Ministry

Spiritual care, chaplaincy, and Bible study for law enforcement officers — supporting those who protect our communities.
